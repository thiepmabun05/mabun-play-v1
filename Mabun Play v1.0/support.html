Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deposit money into my wallet?

Go to the Wallet page and tap "Deposit". You will be redirected to your mobile money app (MTN MoMo or Digitel DigiCash). Follow the prompts to complete the payment. The minimum deposit is 5,000 SSP. Funds usually appear within 1–2 minutes.

How do I withdraw my winnings?

On the Wallet page, tap "Withdraw". Enter the amount (minimum 2,000 SSP, maximum 500,000 SSP per day). A 1% fee applies. Withdrawals are processed instantly to your linked mobile money account.

When do I receive my quiz winnings?

Prizes are credited to your wallet within 30 minutes after the quiz ends. You will receive a notification and the amount will appear in your transaction history.

I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot password". Enter your phone number. You will receive an SMS with a 6-digit code. Enter the code and choose a new password.

Can I change my username or email?

Yes. Go to your Profile and tap the edit icon next to your username or email. Changes take effect immediately. Note that your phone number cannot be changed.

How are quiz winners determined?

Winners are ranked by total score. In case of a tie, we use average answer speed, longest streak, and earliest achievement of score. The top players receive prizes according to the published pool percentages.

What happens if I lose internet during a quiz?

The quiz will continue in the background; your answers are saved locally and submitted when connectivity resumes. However, if the timer expires, the answer is counted as incorrect. We recommend a stable connection.

Is there a fee for using the app?

Registration and browsing are free. Quiz entry fees vary by tier (1,000 SSP for hourly, 2,000 SSP daily, 3,000 SSP weekly, or 15,000 SSP auto-subscribe). Withdrawals incur a 1% fee.
